It seems like you're highlighting the different meanings of the word "bank". Here's a corrected and organized version:

The word "bank" can have different meanings depending on the context:

1. **Riverbank**: The land alongside a river, as in "Down by the riverbank".
2. **Financial institution**: A place where people deposit and withdraw money, as in "Deposit a check at the bank".

This is an example of a homograph, where a single word has multiple meanings and, often, different pronunciations.
